Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Ambimed Healthcare collects and uses information.

Camera Permission

Our app uses the device camera only to allow users to capture images for in-app features such as profile photo or document upload. The camera is never accessed in the background.

Data Collection and Storage

Images captured using the camera are processed only for the intended feature. We do not store, sell, or share camera data with any third party.

User Control

Users can deny or revoke camera permission at any time through their device settings.
